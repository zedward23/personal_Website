About Me

My name is Edward Zhang and I am sophomore at the School of Engineering and Applied Sciences (SEAS) and the Wharton School of Business at the University of Pennsylvania. I am dual-majoring in Digital Media Design (DMD) in SEAS and a Management Concentration at Wharton. I also play Defensive Line for the UPenn Sprint football team, and am a project lead for the game Heroes of Light at the UPenn game development club, UPGRADE.

My dream is to one day revitalize the 2D animation aesthetic by helping to develop a premier shader to perfectly translate the characteristically flat styles of the Disney Renaissance and classical cel-based Anime into CGI. From there, I would work to optmize that technology to lower the bar to entry for creating beautiful animations. With this newfound ease of creation for animation, I hope to scale up the production of high quality animated films and bring to works sporting the 2D aesthetic the same level of prosperity and public attention enjoyed by the modern works from Pixar, Disney, Dreamworks, and Sony.

In my free time, I enjoy drawing, programming, 3D modeling, game development, making video essays about anime, and exercising.
